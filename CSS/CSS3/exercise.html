Word Wrap:
Word wrap comes into action when you have an outstandingly long word. Without it, the word will break outside the box, like so: aifbvgsdfkbvgkstbdfnbvkjnsdgfbkrtb
But with word wrap even when the word is longer than its boundaries it won't break, only drop a line and stay in the defined width: aeodrbgvsekthbgvsrtkbgnskrjtbnkjsrtgbnkrts

Multiple Media Queries
For when you want to define an attribute between a specific range.
For an example, if you want something to happen between 600px to 1000px:
@media(min-width:600px) and (max-width:1000px) {attributes}
