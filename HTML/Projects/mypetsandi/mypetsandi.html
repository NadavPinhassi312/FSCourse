My Pets And I

Blog by Nadav Pinhassi

Welcome to my own personal blog! First things first, hope you got your time machine. If not - it's fine, you just have to pretend we are in 2005, having to do with the font and with the fact that this is written purely with html.
Moving on, this page's subject is my pets. We own a dog and four cats, all adopted from the streets of Israel. My mom and dad agreed on one dog and one cat but the reality thought otherwise and here we are now, with a zoo in an apartment.
They dont always get along well (understatment) but they are all very lovely in their own manner.
